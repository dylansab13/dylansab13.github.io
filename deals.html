Deals

Refer a Friend, Get a Free Ride

With this deal, users can refer their friends to our ride-sharing service and receive a free ride for themselves once their friend completes their first ride. This deal is a great way to incentivize our users to spread the word about our service and bring in new customers. By offering a free ride, we're able to show our appreciation to our loyal users and encourage them to continue using our service.

Weekend Getaway Package

With this deal, users can book a weekend getaway package that includes a round-trip ride to their destination, as well as a discounted hotel stay. This deal is a great way to encourage our users to take a break and enjoy a weekend getaway without having to worry about transportation. By partnering with hotels, we're able to offer our users a great deal on both transportation and accommodations.

Commuter Pass

With this deal, users can purchase a commuter pass that gives them a set number of rides per week or month for a discounted price. This deal is a great way to incentivize our users who use our service for their daily commute to purchase a pass and save money. By offering a discounted price, we're able to encourage our users to use our service more frequently and build loyalty to our brand.

Airport Shuttle Service

With this deal, users can book a shuttle service to and from the airport at a discounted rate. This deal is a great way to provide our users with a reliable and convenient transportation option when traveling. By offering a discounted rate, we're able to encourage our users to use our service for airport transportation instead of more expensive options like taxis or rental cars.

Happy Hour Rides

With this deal, users can book a ride during designated "happy hour" times at a discounted rate. This deal is a great way to encourage our users to use our service during off-peak times and help to balance out demand. By offering a discounted rate, we're able to incentivize our users to use our service during times when we might not have as many rides booked.
